Pedals in Helix

213 Pedals are here for you! Plug-in guitar and modify your sound your way! Place pedals anywhere on the sound path and EXPERIMENT till you get what you want.

All the pedals in Helix LT devided in several logical groups for your convenience. Many pedals in the list where created by Line6 spesialists from scratch and some impressive amount of pedals where copied from existing and popular pedals of different manifacturers.

Every pedal's settings are adjustable and you can monitor changes you make while playing. Parameters are different for ecery pedal, but basicaly all of the have Level parameter.

All pedals in Helix devided in categories such as: Modulation, Dynamics, Distotrion, Delay, Reverb, EQ, Pitch/Synth, Filter, Wah and Volume/Pan. Most of the pedals can function in Mono or Stereo mode depending on your preferences and sound system possibilities.
