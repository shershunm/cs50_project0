Amps in Helix


Helix is stacked with 83 amp models both for the guitar and for bass. Plug in you instrument and dial the sound of your guitar Hero! Rock the room with clean or bright sound and become your own rock-star!

Helix allows you do not use its wast lybrary of build-in amp models. You can just build a path from pedals of your choice and then coonect Helix to your favorite amp-in-a-flesh. And it will sound great. Moreover you can use Helix's Send/Return bus to get your perfect sound.

But lets be honest - to have at your fingertip the opportunity to have not 1, not 2, not even a dozen, but 83 freeking Amps! I think this is awesome!

And imagine how much money you would spend to buy even 3 or 4 amps. Helix lets you save your budget but not the sound! You will have warmth of the tube amp-head, you can even change sattings of the tube and push it to the limits you would never push the real amp. Helix LT allows you to have high quality sound, adjastible parameters, and you can have in your sound path even few different amps working separatly or combined.

Line6 team did a great job modeling all those amps from different brands, some models that are no longer in produce. You can have all the sound from jazzy to heavy metal and all the rest in between.

But Helix LT also has its own, build by Line6 virtual amps that never existed but they bring you spectacular sound for every music ganre.

To go and try all the amps with different settings will take you, probably, few days. And compining the sound with pedals will get you the sound like on the huge stage. You can easily sound like Hendix, Brian May, Eddie Van Halen or Metallica. Thit gear can make your music dreams come true!
