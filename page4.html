Cabs and Mics in Helix


Enjoy pure sound out-of-the-box with sets of best in industry cabinets and mics. Create your own combination and sound like a PRO!


As you may know - you need a good cabinet and good mic in order to get a good sound. When you play a guitar in your room or in small settings - you will only need your cabinet, but when recording or performing in large room or a hall - you will need a good mic to get your sound to the people in auditorium. Sound engineer will then create a perfect mix for people to enjoy your creativity.

Helix LT gives you a good stack of different cabs and a large field for experimenting. It has single or double stack of cabs. They could have up to four speakers each. Speakers have different diameter and manufacturers. And you can pair them with different amps to achieve the sound you are looking for.

Here you can find Cabs models devided by the number of speakers in them.
To get the sound from your cabinet to the recording device or to the sound mixer you will need a right mic. If you are a travelling artist - you will need a spare mic or couple other mics depending on kind of sound you will need for different songs.

There few differnt types of mics used today for this purpose. There are Condencer mics, Dynamic and Ribbon mics. Each type reads and transmitts sound differently. Positioning mics at the Cab wil also play its role in getting the sound. Here comes into play both position concerning the speaker (to the center, to the side, in between speakers) and how far from the speaker you will place the mic.

Helix LT lets you configure all these parameters including imulating the room size. And... you do not have to buy all these mics and cabs as you have them with you all the time you have your Helix LT.
